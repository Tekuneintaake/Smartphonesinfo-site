Why the Nokia 3210 is Legendary

First released in 1999, the Nokia 3210 became an icon known for its durability, week-long battery life, and the addictive game Snake.

Key Specifications

DesignUnbreakable polycarbonate shell
BatteryUp to 1 week (original) / 2-3 days (2024 4G model)
Display1.5" monochrome (original) / 2.4" color (2024)
Connectivity2G (original) / 4G + Bluetooth (2024)
ExtrasSnake game, FM radio, flashlight
Pros: Unmatched durability, no distractions, nostalgic fun.
Cons: No apps, basic camera (2024: 2MP).

The 2024 Comeback Edition

Nokia revived the 3210 with modern touches while keeping its soul:

  • 4G LTE: Faster calls and texts
  • USB-C: No more proprietary chargers
  • Snake in Color: Now with 16-bit graphics!
