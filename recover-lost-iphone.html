How to Recover a Lost iPhone

Home | Find My iPhone | iPhone Buying Guide

Losing your iPhone can feel like a nightmare — but Apple has built-in tools to help you locate it quickly and safely. This guide will walk you through everything you need to do to recover your iPhone, protect your data, and take action if it's stolen.

Step 1: Use Find My iPhone

Visit iCloud.com/find or open the Find My app on another device. After signing in:

  • Select your lost iPhone from the list
  • See its last known location on the map
  • Tap Play Sound to locate it nearby

Step 2: Mark Your iPhone as Lost

Still can’t find it? Mark it as lost to:

  • Lock the device with a passcode
  • Display a custom recovery message on the screen
  • Track any movement or location changes

Step 3: Report to Authorities and Carrier

If stolen, report it to your local police. Then, contact your carrier to:

  • Disable the SIM card to prevent misuse
  • Possibly blacklist the phone

Step 4: Erase Your iPhone Remotely (if needed)

If recovery is unlikely, use Find My to remotely erase your iPhone. This will delete all content and settings, keeping your data safe.

Prevent It in the Future: Enable Find My

  1. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Find My
  2. Turn on Find My iPhone
  3. Enable Send Last Location
