Smartphone Buying Tips

  • Know What You Need: Avoid overpaying for specs you won’t use. Focus on your daily needs.
  • Battery Life Matters: A 5000mAh+ battery helps you last through the day with ease.
  • Software Updates: Choose brands that offer at least 2 years of Android support.
  • Real User Reviews: Check reviews and feedback before buying — specs don’t always tell the full story.
